## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Q: Can employers view my certificate without login?
**A:** Yes! Public certificate pages require no login and can be viewed by anyone with the link.

### Q: What if I want to hide my certificate?
**A:** Contact support to make your certificate private. This will disable the public URL.

### Q: Can I get a new certificate ID?
**A:** Certificate IDs are permanent and tied to your verification attempt. Retake the verification to earn a new certificate.

### Q: Does the link expire?
**A:** No, public certificate links are permanent and valid indefinitely.

### Q: Can I share the PDF instead?
**A:** Yes! You can download and share the PDF. However, the public URL is preferred because:
- Employers can verify authenticity
- Shows real-time blockchain verification
- Includes interactive elements
- Cannot be altered or forged
